Facts about dogs

Dogs have unique nose prints like human fingerprints, a sense of smell up to 100,000 times better than ours, and can learn over 100 words, making them as smart as toddlers; they also dream, sweat through their paws, and puppies are born blind and deaf.

Dogs are color-blind. They have trouble seeing shades of red and green but can see blue and yellow.
